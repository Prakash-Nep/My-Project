Fact Sheet: President Donald J. Trump Imposes Tariffs on Imports from Canada, Mexico and China

President Donald Trump imposed new tariffs on imports from Canada, Mexico, and China in 2025, citing border security and fentanyl concerns. The moves triggered retaliation from Canada and Mexico, disrupted trade negotiations, and raised consumer prices. Over 85% of trade remains tariff-free under USMCA exemptions.

SoftBank Approves $22.5 Billion Second Installment to Complete $30 Billion Investment in OpenAI

SoftBank Group has reportedly approved a second installment of $22.5 billion to finalize its massive $30 billion investment in OpenAI, according to a report by The Information. The Japanese conglomerate’s board has given conditional approval, requiring OpenAI to complete a corporate restructuring that could pave the way for a future public offering.

US President Donald Trump stated India is completely stopping Russian oil purchases. He also mentioned China will significantly reduce its Russian oil imports. The US views India's oil purchases as financing the Ukraine war.
